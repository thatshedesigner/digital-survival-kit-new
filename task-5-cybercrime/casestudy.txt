UPI fraud is a common cybercrime in India where attackers trick users into sending money or revealing sensitive information.

Typically, the fraud begins with a phone call, message, or fake link. The attacker pretends to be from a bank or service provider and asks the user to share an OTP or scan a QR code. Once the user does this, money is deducted from their account.

Students and new users are often targeted due to lack of awareness. The consequences include financial loss and stress.

Such frauds are increasing due to the growth of digital payments and lack of digital awareness.
